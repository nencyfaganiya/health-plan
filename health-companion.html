Health tips
⚠️
Book your GP appointmentAsk for Vitamin D, B12 & Iron blood test. Mention the anal fissure needs topical cream (diltiazem).
🌿 Replace Strigol naturally
Take 1 tbsp Isabgol in warm water every night. Your permanent natural replacement for Strigol. Sat Isabgol or Dabur brand from any Indian grocery.
💧 Water first, tea second
Drink 1 full glass of warm water before morning tea. Tea with milk mildly dehydrates. Water first protects your gut all day.
🚶 Stand every hour at work
You sit 8–5. Hourly reminders are set on weekdays. Stand, walk 2 mins, drink water. Back pain and fatigue reduce within 2 weeks.
🧘 5-min morning stretch
Cat-cow: hands and knees, arch up then dip down, 10 times. Then hug both knees to chest. Decompresses your spine before work every day.
🌙 Dinner by 7:30–8pm
Eating at 9:30pm and sleeping at 10:30pm = 1 hour to digest. Lighter, earlier dinner is the biggest single change for your stomach size.
🚶 10-min walk after dinner
Short walk after dinner dramatically improves digestion and reduces overnight bloating. Even walking at home counts. Do it before sitting.
✨ Get your blood tested
Vitamin D and B12 deficiency are extremely common for Indian-origin people in the UK. They cause fatigue, dull skin, and muscle pain. Free NHS test — book it this week.
😴 Screens off 30 mins before sleep
Instagram and TV blue light disrupts melatonin. This is causing your morning grogginess. Put the phone down at 10pm — your mornings will transform.
